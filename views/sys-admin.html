A system administrator is generally the next position available after a person has experience providing desktop support. System administrators are responsible for a variety of tasks including managing servers, managing patching, backups as well as the overall administration of the network infrastructure. The system administrator will have a higher level of responsibility over a desktop support technician and generally additional knowledge is required.

Individuals looking to be a system administrator can also look into getting certifications such as MCSE (Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert), and RHCE (Red Hat Certified Engineer).

Salary: $66,231 - $90,012
